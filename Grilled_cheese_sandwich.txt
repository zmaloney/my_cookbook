Grilled cheese sandwich

Ingredients : 
2 slices white bread
1 slice Swiss cheese
1 slice cheddar cheese
Butter

Preparation : 
Heat a cast-iron pan on medium 
Toast one side of each slice of bread
Arrange Swiss cheese and cheddar cheese on toasted side of bread, and assemble sandwich with untoasted side in
Butter outside of sandwich and toast on cast-iron pan until golden brown
